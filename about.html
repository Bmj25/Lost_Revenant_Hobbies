About Me

I have been building, painting, and playing strategy tabletop wargames (like Warhammer 40,000) for nearly twenty-three years. This page has been created to present the hobby that I have enjoyed partaking in since 1998 at a HobbyTownUSA in Clarksville, TN.


In recent years, I have enjoyed the journey of honing my painting preparation and painting skills for the models. The major break-through was after I watched a few Next Level Painting YouTube videos and got smart on airbrushing. Kenny's videos helped me to learn the basics and I got to my comfortable display level after working through some private lessons with him.

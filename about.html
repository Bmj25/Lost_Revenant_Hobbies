About Me


I have been building, painting, and playing strategy tabletop wargames (like Warhammer 40,000) for nearly twenty-three years. This page has been created to present the hobby that I have enjoyed partaking in since 1998 at a HobbyTownUSA in Clarksville, TN.


In recent years, I have enjoyed the journey of honing my painting preparation and painting skills for the models. The major break-through was after I watched a few Next Level Painting YouTube videos and got smart on airbrushing. Kenny's videos helped me to learn the basics and get comfortable with my style through some private tutoring.


Excited to share the journey with you! Select one of the pages on navigation bar above to see how I have resourced the war effort. See you on the table!

3D Printing Customization

Branching into resin SLA 3D-Printing has revolutionized the hobby for me. There is nothing better than designing custom conversions and printing them into reality. Having tried a few different printing technologies and brands, I prefer the consistency and relative ease that the Elegoo Mars and Saturn bring to the fight. After dialing in the settings, you really can't go wrong with Water-washable Elegoo resin, a UV curing chamber (like the old-school ANYCUBIC Wash and Cure 1.0), Chitubox Slicer, and one of the printers.

I highly recommend searching for STLs on Yeggi or Cults3D. Once you find creators that you routinely see, please consider supporting them. It is the least we can do for those developing such stellar designs!

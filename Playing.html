Useful Gaming and Hobby Links


  • Warhammer Community - The official website for Warhammer 40,000's community engagement. I appreciate it for daily content.
  • Wahapedia - This website is a great online resource for rules clarification.
  • Battlescribe - This website has the the preferred phone app for building armies and submitting lists for events
  • Goonhammer - This website's team provides fantastic analysis and tacticas for emerging friends and foes!
  • The Long War - Kenny, Wyatt, and team for The Long War Podcast. I recommend these guys as the best in the community, extremely skilled and just all around great people!
  • Faeit 212 - This website is the OG rumormill. I have been following this page for years.
